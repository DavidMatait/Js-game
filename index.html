Black Jack

Rules of Black Jack

  • Bigger display represents current value of your card
  • Smaller screen represents total value of your cards
  • Press Hit to add value of the card
  • If you reach more than 21 points, you loose
  • Once you are satisfied with total value, press settle
  • Settle, makes upper screen run dealers cards
  • If dealer total value is greater than yours, you loose, if equal, then draw, if your total value is greater than dealers but less than 21, you win the game
